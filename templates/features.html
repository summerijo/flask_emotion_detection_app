Feature Section

The platform features two primary modes of operation: real-time and photo-based emotion recognition.

  Real-time Detection

In the real-time mode, users can activate their device's camera and position their faces within a designated rectangle area on the screen. The platform then analyzes facial expressions captured by the camera in real-time, providing immediate feedback on the emotional states detected. This feature enables users to gain insight into their own emotions or those of others in live interactions, enhancing self-awareness and interpersonal communication skills.

  Image-Based Detection

In addition, the platform offers a photo-based emotion recognition feature, allowing users to upload images for detailed analysis. Upon uploading a photo, the platform uses its advanced algorithms to examine facial features and expressions, identifying and categorizing the emotions depicted in the image. This functionality facilitates deeper exploration of emotions and provides users with valuable insights into the emotional content of images. Whether used for personal reflection, research purposes, or practical applications in fields such as psychology and marketing, the photo-based feature extends the platform's utility and enhances its accessibility to users seeking to understand and interpret human emotions.
